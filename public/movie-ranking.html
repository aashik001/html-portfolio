The Best Movies According to Amisatoshi

My top 3 movies of all time.

Akira

This is my favourite anime/manga film that introduced me to the genre.

The Godfather Part 2

The best gangster movie of all time. The second movie in the trilogy is the best one, featuring Al Pacino and Robert De Niro.

Bladerunner - The Final Cut

The OG cyberpunk movie of all time. This particular version is the best version after the director's cut.
